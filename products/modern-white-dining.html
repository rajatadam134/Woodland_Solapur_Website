Modern White Marble Dining Table

Introduce a clean, modern aesthetic to your dining room with our Modern White Marble Dining Table. Showcasing a pristine white quartz/marble table top with soft rounded corners, it is supported by a solid oak wood structural frame and flared tapered legs. Paired with elegant armless dining chairs in sand-beige upholstery.

Inquire for Price (WhatsApp)
Materials & Craftsmanship

White quartz/marble engineered slab with polished protective coating. Table underframe and tapered legs handcrafted from solid oak wood. Armless dining chairs upholstered in sand-beige canvas fabric.

Dimensions & Details

Table Width: 160 cm | Table Depth: 90 cm | Table Height: 75 cm. Includes 6 custom-designed armless dining chairs.

Care Instructions

Wipe with a soft damp cloth. Avoid dragging ceramic tableware directly on the polished quartz surface; use placemats and runners. Clean fabric chairs with gentle upholstery foam.
