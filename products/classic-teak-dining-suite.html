Classic Teak Dining Suite

Bring timeless sophistication to your family meals with our Classic Teak Dining Suite. Hand-carved from seasoned premium solid teak wood, this set features a rectangular dining table with custom detailed borders, matched with six elegant high-back chairs designed for supportive comfort.

Materials & Craftsmanship

Hand-selected sustainable seasoned solid teak wood. Assembled with traditional woodwork joinery for superior stability. Finished in high-clarity natural matte polyurethane sealant.

Dimensions & Details

Table Width: 180 cm | Table Depth: 90 cm | Table Height: 76 cm. Includes six high-back dining chairs with contoured wooden slatted backs.

Care Instructions

Wipe clean using a soft microfiber cloth slightly dampened with lukewarm water. Use coasters and table pads under hot bowls and cups. Avoid contact with solvent cleaners.
