Office Executive Ergonomic Chair

Command productivity in comfort with our Office Executive Ergonomic Chair. Carefully curated for professional board rooms and executive office suites, this task chair features active lumbar alignment support, breathable mesh backing, a contoured seat cushion, and fully adjustable height and tilt dynamics.

Materials & Construction

High-tensile breathable nylon mesh backrest. Contoured molded foam seat cushion with premium fabric finish. Reinforced five-star star base with smooth rolling nylon casters.

Dimensions & Details

Height: 115-125 cm (Adjustable) | Width: 65 cm | Seat Depth: 52 cm. Pneumatic Class 4 gas lift for seamless adjustment. Assembly tools and guide included.

Care Instructions

Vacuum fabric and mesh periodically. Wipe down armrests and base using a soft damp cloth. Check and tighten connector screws once a year.
