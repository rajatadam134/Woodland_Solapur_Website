Classic Teak Bed Set

Establish a majestic sanctuary with our Classic Teak Bed Set. Materials & Craftsmanship

Seasoned A-Grade solid teak wood structure. Padded headboard upholstered in premium plum-purple organic blend cotton fabric. Natural teak finish.

Dimensions & Details

Frame Width: 195 cm (King size) | Length: 215 cm | Headboard Height: 120 cm. Includes two smooth-rolling under-bed storage bays.

Care Instructions

Wipe wood panels with a clean damp cloth, followed by dry microfiber. Vacuum the headboard fabric with light brush attachments. Keep away from direct water contact.
