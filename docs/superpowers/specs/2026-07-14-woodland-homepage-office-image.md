# Design Specification: Homepage Office Collection Image Replacement (July 14, 2026)

## Purpose & Scope
This specification describes replacing the Office Suite collection image (`cat_office.jpg`) on the homepage curated collections section. The current image feels too fictional/AI-generated. We will generate a realistic, warm, high-end catalog photograph of a teak executive suite that aligns with the organic, warm sand-beige and espresso theme of the Living Room and Bedroom showcases.

---

## 1. Style & Image Asset Specifications

*   **Asset Filename**: `assets/images/cat_office.jpg`
*   **Resolution / Aspect Ratio**: `4:3`
*   **Visual Direction**:
    *   *Subject*: A premium executive office suite containing a handcrafted solid teak office desk and an ergonomic leather executive chair.
    *   *Aesthetics*: Warm natural side light casting soft, long shadows. A luxurious, calm corporate office setting with sand-beige walls and espresso wood accents.
    *   *Style*: High-end architectural digest / professional furniture catalog photography. No over-saturated AI colors, no floating holographic UI, and no futuristic elements. Matches the organic, real-world style of `cat_living.jpg` and `cat_bedroom.jpg`.
